About this project

Aer·Aqua

Aer·Aqua is an open environmental monitoring dashboard for the municipalities of Kordelio and Evosmos in the Thessaloniki metropolitan area. It aggregates eight years of daily air-quality measurements for five key pollutants and combines them with municipal water-quality reports from ΕΥΑΘ.

The goal is to make environmental data transparent and accessible to residents, researchers, and local authorities. This website is developed by MAKRIDIS POLYCHRONIS AM:2022100
